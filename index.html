Finger Mapping

  • Left Ring: S, W, X
  • Left Middle: D, E, C

Typing Tips

  • Keep your back straight and relaxed
  • Keep your wrists straight, not bent
  • Keep your feet flat on the floor
  • Eyes should be on the text, not the keyboard
  • Use all fingers, not just a few
